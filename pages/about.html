[//] What Drives Me Toward Security

Technology has always been something I gravitate toward — not just as a tool, but as something worth understanding from the inside out. Growing up watching it evolve at a pace that never stopped being interesting, that curiosity never really left.

Cyberpunk 2077 genuinely influenced the direction. Not because of the aesthetic — though that clearly stuck — but because it put a spotlight on what it means for systems to be compromised, exploited, and defended in a world completely dependent on technology. That idea resonated. The real world isn't far off.

What draws me to security is the purpose behind it — actively protecting organizations from threats that are real, evolving, and consequential. It's not a passive field. That kind of meaningful, high-stakes work is exactly where I want to be.
